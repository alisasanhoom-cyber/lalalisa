What We Look For

👤

Open to All Levels

We welcome beginners through to working professionals. Attitude and coachability matter more than a full portfolio.

📏

Diverse Looks

No strict height minimums. We represent diverse looks across commercial, fashion, lifestyle, and acting categories.

🌏

Based in Thailand

Ideal candidates are Bangkok-based or visiting for 2+ weeks. Models in Phuket, Chiang Mai, and Koh Samui also considered.

📅

Availability

Minimum availability of 2 days per month required. More availability means more bookings and faster roster growth.

Your Application

Upload a clear, recent photo. JPEG or PNG, max 10MB. Natural light preferred — no heavy filters.

We review all applications within 5–7 business days. If shortlisted, we'll contact you to arrange a go-see at our Bangkok office.
