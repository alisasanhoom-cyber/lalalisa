Usage Rights

All bookings include a standard license covering use of images/footage in the specific project agreed at booking (e.g. one campaign, one social media post series).

Extended usage — TV broadcast, international campaigns, long-term billboard use, or full buyout — carries an additional usage fee negotiated per project.

We advise all clients to confirm intended usage in the casting brief so we can include appropriate usage rights in the initial quote.
