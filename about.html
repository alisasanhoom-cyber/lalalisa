About Us

Est. Bangkok · 2017

Who We Are

Morgan & Preston Models is an international Modeling and Talent agency based in Bangkok, Thailand — at the heart of Asia's creative industry.

Since our founding, we've been committed to shaping the future of fashion and entertainment by discovering bold, global talents and elevating them onto the world stage. With a strong pulse on Asian and international markets, we specialize in scouting distinctive faces and cultivating their potential through personalized guidance, creative direction, and strategic industry connections.

Our models have been featured in global campaigns, editorials, runway shows, and top-tier brand collaborations. As one of Asia's leading agencies, MP Models continues to set the standard for professionalism, innovation, and excellence in the industry.
