Training & Workshops

Bangkok-based professional development for models at every level

Develop Your Career

Whether you're just starting out or looking to sharpen professional skills, our Bangkok workshops are run by working industry professionals with over a decade of hands-on experience placing models in campaigns for global brands.

We offer focused half-day and full-day workshops covering runway technique, on-camera commercial acting, portfolio building, and full agency preparation. Small groups kept to a maximum of 8 participants to ensure personal coaching.

Workshops are conducted in English. Participants receive a certificate of completion and a personal feedback report from the trainer.

What We Offer

🚶

Runway & Movement

Catwalk technique, body movement and control, posing transitions, and working with photographers on set.

🎭

Commercial Acting

On-camera confidence, taking direction from a director, natural expression, and emotional range for TV commercial work.

📸

Portfolio Day

A full professional photo shoot with multiple outfits and looks. Digital files delivered within 5 working days.

💼

Agency Prep

How the industry works in Bangkok, comp card creation, booking process, rates, contracts, and how to approach agencies.
